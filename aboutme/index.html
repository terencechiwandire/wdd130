About Me

Terence Chiwandire

My name is Terence Chiwandire. I am from Johannesburg, South Africa. I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints for about 20 years.
I served my mission in 2008 and returned in 2010. I have been married to my beautiful wife for 6 years, and we have 2 beautiful girls, Nakai and Tawana aged
4 years and 1 year, respectively.

I love being in the outdoors. My favorite activity is playing soccer, especially with the brethren from my Stake. We play soccer every Saturday morning and I look
forward to this every day of the week. I love meeting new people and gegtting to know them. I look forward to meeting everyone in this course with me as we learn
together.
